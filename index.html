Most famous types of continental food items

What is continental food ?

Continental food encompasses delicacies found in European countries. Indian and Chinese cuisines are essentially not a part of continental platter rather it consists of French and Mediterranean food. You would find sauces being used as a seasoning in continental food and the cooking techniques would be confined to roasting, frying, and baking.

French Toast

French toast is the go-to breakfast meal of many people in the European countries and the United States. It’s prepared by soaking bread slices in beaten eggs and milk. The bread is then pan-fried and topped with maple syrup and berries.It’s also consumed as a side dish served alongside bacon and scrambled eggs. If you haven’t tried the French Toast yet, go ahead and try this impeccable toast once. You’ll ditch your normal buttered toast.

Chicken Sandwich

Chicken Sandwich is filled with the goodness of boneless chicken put between cheese slices, lettuce, veggies, and a lot of sauces. It’s the easiest recipe one can make to fulfill their hunger pangs. You can experiment with this dish by opting for buns instead of bread slices or making your own version with different sauces. Chicken Sandwich is made in no time. Non-vegetarians stick to this meal when they are broke or running late.

Pancakes

Pancakes are known to everyone. They don’t need an introduction because a majority of the people have them as their breakfast meal. Ideally, pancakes are made from a starch-based batter that consists of eggs, milk, and butter but nowadays, this flat round cake goes through a lot of kitchen experiments. From Rainbow Pancakes to Hot Chocolate Pancakes, you can experiment a lot with this continental dish. It’s topped with maple syrup, whipped creams, and berries. Overall, pancakes taste heavenly and can’t be replaced by any sweet breakfast option.

Caesar Salad

Continental cuisine has a lot to do with salads and wonderful dressings. One such salad is Caesar Salad. It was invented by a magnificent Italian chef, Caesar Cardini. Caesar Salad is a green salad with croutons and romaine lettuce. The dressing is prepared from olive oil, parmesan cheese, garlic, lemon juice, and egg. It looks very wholesome and nutritious.
